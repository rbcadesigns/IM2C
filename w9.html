HUMAN EFFECT

The interactive digital wall installation reflects on the impact that humans of on the environment around them – when a human passes by, the foliage gradually deteriorates.

BEINGS

Universal Everything

Using a combination of video game engines, AI algorithms and VFX software, the art and design collective, Universal Everything, have created an interactive and digitally generative artwork in which creatures become increasingly animated based on the viewer’s own interaction and movement in the space.

SOURCE

AD INFINITUM

Hasso Plattner Institute (Potsdam, Germany)

In a subversion of the usual human-computer interaction dynamic, Ad Infinitum is a parasitical entity that lives off the involuntary kinetic energy of the human as their muscles are trapped by cuffs and electrically stimulated to perform a cranking motion.

FIRMATA LIVE DEMO~ WAIT WOT?

WHAT IS A FIRMATA?

A Firmata is a device control available in the Arduino library that allows communication between physical computing Arduino set ups and host computer software.










Karen Ann demonstrated the use of a Firmata to us in this week's class in the form of a twistable knob that was controlling the size of a p5.js circle on the laptop screen.
