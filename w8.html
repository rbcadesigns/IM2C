SPAMBOTS

Neil Mendoza

Animatronic Spam collectively type out an AI-generated script based on Aldous Huxley’s novel, Brave New World which invite the audience to read the text on screen.

REVEALUXION

Ki-Eun Kim

Inspired by the form and function of cocoons and marionettes, Revealuxion is an experimental performance garment in which the artist’s fingers control the opening to the chrysalis they are positioned within.

MATERIAL EXPRESSIONS NO.2

Laura Splan

Material Expressions No. 2 is as a durational performance piece in which the heartbeat of the performer activates the unravelling of the yarn holder motor, tracked through the use of Electromyography (EMG) electrodes attached to the chest of the performer.
